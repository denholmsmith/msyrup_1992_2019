Data source: https://www.uvm.edu/femc/data/archive/project/sugarmaple_production/dataset/maple-syrup-production/overview

Variable names and descriptions:

Program = data collection method
Year = year according to gregorian calendar
Geo_Level = level of greophical information
State = state within the US from which the data were collected
State_NAS = American National Standards Institute (ANSI) Codes for States
Commodity = ommodity produced
ProductionInDollars = amount of commodity sold in United States Dollar (USD)
ProductionInGallons = amount of commodity produced in gallons
PriceReceivedDollarsPerGallon = USD received per gallon of commodity sold
